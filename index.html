Core Features

Everything you need,
in one place.

Built for how Gen Z actually discovers and attends events — no more scattered Instagram pages or WhatsApp rumours.

🗺️
Centralized Event Discovery

Every event in one place. Concerts, tech summits, campus nights, book clubs — browse all categories without jumping between platforms or hoping someone tagged you.

🎉
🎟️
In-App Ticket Purchase

Buy tickets without leaving OUTSYDE. Secure payments via Paystack & Flutterwave, instant digital delivery, and QR codes for entry — zero third-party chaos.

🤖
AI-Driven Recommendations

OUTSYDE learns your taste. The more you use it, the smarter your feed gets — events picked for you, not just for everyone.

📍
Nearby Events Feature

Discover events happening around your current location. Spontaneous plans just became a lot easier.

🔁
Ticket Transfer & Resale

Can't make it? Securely transfer or resell your ticket within the app. No shady DMs, no money lost, no risk.

🏫
Campus Events Feature

A dedicated space for student life — departmental events, fellowships, tech clubs, social hangouts. Campus culture, finally organized.

✨
Personalized Interest Feed

Set your vibe at signup. OUTSYDE filters the noise and shows you exactly what matches your interests — nothing irrelevant, ever.

🔔
Real-Time Notifications

Get instant alerts on events dropping near you, last-chance tickets, and lineup updates. FOMO is officially cancelled.

🔐
Verified & Secure Access

Every event and organizer is verified before going live. SSL encryption, QR authentication, and fraud detection — built in, not bolted on.

How It Works

From scroll to
front row.

Getting to your next event should take seconds, not hours of group chats and guesswork.

01
📲
Download & Set Your Interests

Sign up and tell OUTSYDE what you're into. Music, tech, sports, culture — your feed is personalized from day one.

02
🔍
Discover Events

Browse your curated feed, search by category or location, or let the AI surface what you'll love before you even look.

03
💳
Buy Your Ticket Securely

Tap, pay, done. Secure in-app payment via Paystack or Flutterwave. Your digital ticket lands instantly — no links, no DMs, no stress.

04
🎉
Show Up & Enjoy

Scan your QR code at the door and walk in. Can't make it? Transfer or resell your ticket inside the app.
